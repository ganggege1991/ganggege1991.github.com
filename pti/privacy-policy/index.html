Privacy Policy

How we protect your privacy

Overview

PTI is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

Data Collection

Local Processing

PTI processes all PDF files locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your files or personal information to any servers.

No Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Names or contact information
  • Device information
  • Usage statistics
  • PDF files or converted images

Data Security

Since all processing is done locally on your device:

  • Your files never leave your device
  • No data is stored on external servers
  • No internet connection is required for the core functionality
